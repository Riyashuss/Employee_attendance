
Description:
The Employee Attendance Management App is a digital solution designed to automate and simplify the process of recording, tracking, and managing employee attendance. 
It eliminates manual registers, increases accuracy, and improves transparency between HR, management, and employees.

 1. Employee Login/Logout
Secure login using email/mobile & password.

Dashboard showing today's attendance, total hours, and leave info.

One-tap check-in and check-out system using:

GPS location

QR Code

Face recognition (optional)


2. Location Tracking (for field employees)
GPS-based attendance ensures the employee is at the correct work location.

Admin can view real-time employee location and working hours.

 3. Attendance History
Daily/Weekly/Monthly attendance summary.

Color-coded calendar view for:

Present

Absent 

Leave 

Holidays

 4. Leave Management
Apply for leave with reason and dates.

Auto-email or app notification to manager for approval.

Track leave balance (CL, SL, PL) and leave history.

 5. Shift & Work Hours Management
Define custom shift timings (Morning, Evening, Night).

Late-in / Early-out tracking.

Overtime calculation.

 6. Admin Panel (For HR & Managers)
View all employee attendance in real-time.

Generate detailed reports:

Daily attendance

Late coming

Monthly summary

Approve/reject leave and regularization requests.

Export reports in Excel/PDF.

 7. Notifications & Alerts
Reminders for check-in and check-out.

Notification for approved/rejected leave.

Alerts for late arrivals or missed attendance.
